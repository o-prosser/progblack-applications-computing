Effects of Computing on Sport

This page will cover the many uses of computing in sport, which range from an athlete's nutrition and training, to enhancing fan experience at live events.

Some notable uses include:

  • Assistant Referee systems (VAR, Hawkeye, UltraEdge)
  • Statistical Tracking Automation
  • Sports Media
  • Sports Video Games
  • Scouting
  • AI Modelling
